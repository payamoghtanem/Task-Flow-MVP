# Sprint Review — Ceremony Script v1.0

## Participants
Product Owner (02) [lead], Scrum Master (03) [facilitator], Validator (14), Tester (13)

## Time Box
Max 1 hour. Sprint end.

## Purpose
Demonstrate completed work, accept or reject stories against Gherkin ACs.

## Agenda

### 1. Sprint Summary (10 min)
- Stories committed vs. stories completed
- Velocity for this sprint
- Any stories not completed and why

### 2. Story Demonstrations (40 min)
For each completed story:
- Demo: Show the running feature (via Tester or Dev)
- AC Check: Validator (14) confirms `VALIDATION-REPORT.md` = PASS
- PO Decision: ACCEPTED or REJECTED
  - ACCEPTED: Story moves to DONE in kanban
  - REJECTED: Story returns to backlog with specific reason

### 3. Metrics Review (10 min)
- Test coverage for this sprint
- Traceability matrix GREEN %
- Security finding count

## Output
`artifacts/sprint-reports/sprint-review-N.md`:
- Stories Accepted: [list]
- Stories Rejected: [list with reasons]
- Velocity: [N] points
- Coverage: [N]%
